Description :
Transforms Blobs into spiders.
You just saw a jumping mushroom , or a jumping snake head , or a jumping eyes cake or a jumping living flower-pot ,
 and you are like "Did i sign for this ?" and i feel you.
 This mod will replace the vanilla blobs, with the vanilla spiders models.
( this mod is compatible with my "blobs attack" mod )
